About Us

IG ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS is a civil and structural engineering consultancy firm that provides essential services to support building projects. We specialize in structural steel design, formwork and falsework design, and temporary work design. Our team also conducts periodic structural inspections to ensure safety and compliance. Additionally, we manage BCA submissions to streamline the approval process for our clients. With a strong focus on quality and client satisfaction, we help bring construction projects to life safely and efficiently.

Our Services

  • Periodic Structural Inspection
  • Civil, Structural, and Geotechnical Engineering Design
  • Temporary Works Design
  • Scaffolding, Formworks, and Falseworks Design
  • Temporary ERSS Design
  • Authority Submission
